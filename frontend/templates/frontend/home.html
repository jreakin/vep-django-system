Features

Voter Data Management

Upload, validate, and manage voter records with CSV processing and data enrichment capabilities.

  • ✓ CSV upload with column mapping
  • ✓ Data validation with Pydantic
  • ✓ Geocoding integration
  • ✓ Social media enrichment
Role-Based Access

Multi-level access control with state, county, candidate, vendor, and volunteer roles.

  • ✓ State and county accounts
  • ✓ Candidate profiles
  • ✓ Vendor access management
  • ✓ Volunteer coordination
Campaign Tools

Comprehensive campaign management with audience targeting and multi-channel outreach.

  • ✓ Audience filtering and segmentation
  • ✓ Multi-platform campaigns
  • ✓ Canvassing and walk lists
  • ✓ Volunteer management
Integrations

Connect with external services for data enrichment and campaign execution.

  • ✓ FullContact, Spokeo, ZoomInfo
  • ✓ Segment analytics
  • ✓ Social media platforms
  • ✓ Email and SMS services
